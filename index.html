👩🏻 Save Mankind 👨🏻

A unique 2D adventure where you will have to... well, save mankind.

Instructions:

Arrow up (⬆️): Your hero will move in the up direction.

Arrow down (⬇️): Your hero will move in the down direction.

🛑 Mini-asteroids: -1 life. Asteroids: -2 lives. UFOs: -3 lives (insta-death). No hearts, no game! 🛑

⚠️ Kryptonite will throw you to the end (or the start) of the screen and remove 5 points from your score. Be careful with it! ⚠️

✌🏻 Touch the hearts to gain an extra life **. Each coin gives you 5 points. 50 points will make you win! ✌🏻

🙃 ** You can have a total of 3 lives. If you touch a heart while you have all of them, you won't earn anything! 🙃
